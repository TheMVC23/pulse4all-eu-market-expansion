Denmark · top-line
Subscription-mature · Falck dominates 3yr lock-ins · First-8 closest like-for-like at 62% above Pulse4all
Denmark is the most subscription-mature of the three Nordic markets. Falck dominates the brand-prestige tier with three published yearly tiers (4,375-6,580 DKK) on 3-year bindings. First-8 is the closest direct subscription competitor at 592 DKK/mo ex VAT all-in. The mid-market (Genopliv, LifeAid, AEDbutikken) sells device + Bronze/Silver/Gold/Platinum service ladders — everyone publishes prices, transparency is the norm. Dansk Folkehjælp owns the charity and community-volunteer angle. Pulse4all wedge: at €29.95/mo on a 1-month rolling contract, Pulse4all undercuts First-8 by 62%, undercuts Falck's cheapest tier by 39%, and matches Genopliv-grade aftercare at half the 36-month TCO.

Top 6 — Denmark

#1 · The Danish emergency brand · €49-74/mo on 3-yr binding

Falck

www.falck.dk/erhverv/forstehjaelp
"Pålidelig og sikker hjertestarter" — reliable and safe AED, with corporate first-aid packages.
What's bundled
AED on subscription (3-yr binding) with reservedelsaftale (parts agreement, min 2-yr), training (Grundkursus i førstehjælp med hjertestarter), AED cabinets, battery-change guides, customer self-service portal.
Messaging themes
Pålidelig og sikker Kan jeres medarbejdere førstehjælp? Hvor skal jeres hjertestarter hænge? Reservedelsaftale inkluderet Falck — trygheden i Danmark
Pricing transparency
Fully published Three tiers: 4,375 DKK/yr (~€49/mo), 5,795 DKK/yr (~€65/mo), 6,580 DKK/yr (~€73/mo, fully automatic). All ex VAT, all on 3-yr binding (totals up to 19,740 DKK on 3 years).
Trust signals
Falck — Denmark's emergency-response brand, instantly recognised. 70 25 70 73 sales line. Public-sector and corporate references. Self-service portal.
Distinguishing features
Brand. Falck's ambulance and roadside-rescue association is unmatched anywhere in Denmark. Combines AED with broader corporate emergency-prep packages.
Pulse4all wedge
Falck's cheapest tier is €49/mo on a 3-yr lock-in vs Pulse4all's €29.95 on 1-month rolling. Over 36 months a Falck customer pays ≈ 13,125-19,740 DKK; a Pulse4all customer pays ≈ 8,030 DKK. Falck wins on brand; Pulse4all wins on flexibility and on >35% lower TCO. Pitch: 'Falck-grade aftercare, half the cost, no 3-year handcuffs.'
#2 · Active ambulance medics · industry association member

LifeAid

www.lifeaid.dk
"Vi klær' dig på til førstehjælp! — Aktive ambulancereddere" — active ambulance medics dressing you for first aid.
What's bundled
Device sales (DefiSign Life, DefiSign Life Online, Heart Guardian HR-501), service agreements in Sølv/Guld tiers (435-735 DKK per cycle), pads & battery managed, hjertestarter.dk registration, 15% discount on courses, label with service hotline on cabinet, sticker showing next service date, hotline.
Messaging themes
Aktive ambulancereddere Medlem af Brancheforeningen for AED hjertestartere Vi holder styr på udløbsdatoer Registrering på hjertestarter.dk inkluderet 15% rabat på kurser
Pricing transparency
Strong Device prices, service tiers and add-ons fully published.
Trust signals
Industry association membership (Brancheforeningen for AED hjertestartere) · Founder/team identity — actual ambulance personnel · Phone 71 96 71 76.
Distinguishing features
Sends new pads and battery but customer fits them — splits the difference between full-service and DIY. Heaviest first-responder identity in Denmark.
Pulse4all wedge
LifeAid's Sølv tier (~€58/yr) is service-only — device cost is on top. Pulse4all's €29.95/mo wraps device + service. Also: LifeAid sends the parts but the customer installs — Pulse4all dispatches and the customer simply confirms. Lower friction, fully bundled.
#3 · Mid-market price-quality · loaner-on-use included

Genopliv

www.genopliv.dk
"Vi gør virksomheder hjertesikre — høj faglighed, god kvalitet og priser."
What's bundled
Devices (Lifeline VIEW pakke 11,767 DKK, Cardiolife AED-3100 13,349 DKK, range from 9,450 DKK), courses, full service agreement at 1,550 DKK/yr ex VAT — annual technician visit, inspection, cleaning, software updates, pads + battery on expiry & on use, free loaner if used or stolen, 15% course discount.
Messaging themes
Hjertesikre virksomheder Høj faglighed, god kvalitet og priser Fuld serviceaftale 1.550 kr/år Lånehjertestarter ved brug eller tyveri Vi tager hånd om alt
Pricing transparency
Fully published Devices, packages, service all published.
Trust signals
Long-form FAQ, abonnementsbetingelser publicly readable · Multiple device brands (Defibtech, Cardiolife) · Course-discount cross-sell.
Distinguishing features
Free loaner-on-use is genuinely differentiated and not common in DK. Strong mid-market price-quality positioning.
Pulse4all wedge
Genopliv splits the bill: device upfront (9,450-13,349 DKK) plus 1,550 DKK/yr service. 36-month TCO ≈ 14,100-18,000 DKK. Pulse4all's €29.95 over 36 months is ≈ 8,030 DKK — half the cost, zero capex, same loaner-on-use included.
#4 · CLOSEST direct subscription · 592 DKK/mo all-in

First-8

www.first-8.dk
"Hjertestarter — Køb hjertestartere i høj kvalitet" — high-quality AEDs.
What's bundled
Devices (Zoll AED Plus 10,700 DKK ex VAT, full outdoor packages 14,700-22,500 DKK), Komfort A service package 2,192 DKK ex VAT, basic service 2,000 DKK/yr, full outdoor subscription at 592 DKK/mo ex VAT (~€79/mo) covering everything.
Messaging themes
Effektiv, brugervenlig, robust Komfort-servicepakke — den ultimative tryghed Komplet udendørs løsning Den fulde service Hurtig levering
Pricing transparency
Fully published Every tier visible online.
Trust signals
Zoll and Defibtech as core brands · Public service-tier pricing.
Distinguishing features
The 592 DKK/mo all-inclusive outdoor subscription is the closest direct competitor to Pulse4all's model — outdoor cabinet + monitoring + service in one monthly.
Pulse4all wedge
First-8 at 592 DKK/mo (≈ €79) vs Pulse4all at €29.95. Same all-in promise — Pulse4all is 62% cheaper. Pricing wins outright; messaging pitch: 'All-inclusive outdoor AED — at less than half First-8's price.'
#5 · 22 years · Bronze/Sølv/Guld/Platinum service ladder

AEDbutikken

www.aedbutikken.dk
"Den Europæiske hjertestarter specialist" — the European AED specialist, since 2004.
What's bundled
Largest AED selection in Denmark; service tiers Standard, Sølv, Guld, Platinum (Platinum = 'alt inklusiv' — all replacements free incl. on use). Annual physical inspection with shock test.
Messaging themes
Den Europæiske hjertestarter specialist Største sortiment i Danmark 20+ års erfaring Platinum — alt inklusiv Pris fra ca. 8.000-20.000 kr.
Pricing transparency
Mostly published Tier names public, exact tier prices vary by device. Device range explicitly disclosed (8,000-20,000 DKK).
Trust signals
Founded 2004 — 22 years in market · Multi-brand authorised reseller · 'Europe's leading AED specialist' claim.
Distinguishing features
Range breadth — they stock more brands and cabinets than anyone else in DK. Annual shock-test included even in lower tiers.
Pulse4all wedge
AEDbutikken sells the device and adds service on top. Total ownership 12,000-22,000 DKK over 3 years. Pulse4all rewrites the equation: no purchase, no Platinum upsell, €29.95 covers everything. Attack the price-anchoring: 'Why pick a tier when one €30/mo subscription covers what their Platinum does?'
#6 · Charity · 100 years · community AED model

Dansk Folkehjælp

www.folkehjaelp.dk
"Førstehjælp har været en del af vores DNA i mere end 100 år."
What's bundled
Schiller Fred Easy AED (≈ 21,431 DKK with cabinet) or Zoll AED 3, full first-aid course at branch or on-site, post-course AED installation in local community placed per Sundhedsstyrelsen guidance, registration on hjertestarter.dk, 'hjerteløber' volunteer network.
Messaging themes
Danmark Redder Liv Hjertestarter i lokalsamfundet 100 års førstehjælpserfaring Frivillige hjerteløbere Multipulse Biowave-teknologi
Pricing transparency
Mixed Course pricing on website · AED packages quote-driven.
Trust signals
Charity status — the Danish equivalent of Red Cross or LHL. Long history. Nationwide volunteer network. Backed by Sundhedsstyrelsen guidelines.
Distinguishing features
Community-AED model — installed for whole neighbourhood use, not single business. Hjerteløber programme.
Pulse4all wedge
Folkehjælp serves the community / volunteer use case, not the SME workplace one. Pulse4all targets businesses that need their own dedicated, monitored AED with documented compliance — a different buyer. Co-existence pitch, not direct competition.
