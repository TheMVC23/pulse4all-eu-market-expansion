Country snapshot · 60-second read
Sweden · top-line
60-month rental + separate add-ons is the dominant structure · published pricing common · Brandfast = cleanest like-for-like comp
Sweden has the most mature AED reseller market on this list. Pricing transparency is partially established (Brandfast, Hjärtstartarspecialisten, Medisol all publish) but the dominant structure is multi-year rental contracts (60 months) with separate add-ons for service (169 SEK/mo), insurance (250 SEK/yr), and training (paid course). The "Hjärtsäker zon" certification framework (Presto) lends credibility but adds friction. Pulse4all wedge: cancel-anytime vs 60-month lock-in, bundled consumables/training/insurance vs à-la-carte add-ons, instant-checkout vs phone-quote sales motion. Direct numerical advantage on like-for-like ZOLL Plus rentals — Pulse4all €30 vs Brandfast ~€49 stacked.

Top 7 — Sweden

#1 · Global authority brand · clinical training

Laerdal Medical Sverige

laerdal.com/se
"Helping save lives" — global mission line, Swedish hub headline "Hjärtstartare — produkter och tjänster."
What's bundled
Device-led catalogue: AED Trainer 2/3, defibrillation pads, batteries, accessories. Sweden does not sell a complete subscription bundle direct — Laerdal positions as OEM/training equipment supplier and routes service-led customers via partners (HLR Konsulten, prestofirstaid.se). Training simulators and HLR manikins are the volume SKUs.
Messaging themes
Helping save lives Training simulation Competence Evidence-based education Resuscitation 2030
Pricing transparency
Quote-only on hjärtstartare; some accessory list prices visible after partner login.
Trust signals
ILCOR, ERC, AHA partner logos · "Resuscitation 2030" goal · presence in 100+ countries · clinical-research bibliography.
Distinguishing features
The brand reference for clinical training — only player with full ecosystem from Resusci Anne mannequin to AED Trainer 3 to SimMan. Authority capital, not commercial sharpness.
Pulse4all wedge
Laerdal punts SMB customers to dealers; dealers stack 25-40% margin. Pulse4all bypasses both — SMB owner sees one €29.95 line on the invoice, no dealer markup, training included via portal. Laerdal's authority is fine for hospitals; for the bakery, the gym, the dental practice, Pulse4all is the answer.
#2 · Multi-service facility incumbent · "Hjärtsäker zon"

Presto

presto.se
"Hjärtsäker zon" — "Become a certified Heart Safe Zone." Sub-line: "Allt för att rädda liv."
What's bundled
Modular: AED device + HLR utbildning (CPR training course) + service agreement + cabinet sold separately. Sweden's largest fire-safety/first-aid services company — AEDs ride alongside extinguishers and first-aid stations on a single facility-services contract.
Messaging themes
Komplett lösning Hjärtsäker zon certifiering Trygghet på arbetsplatsen Sveriges ledande inom första hjälpen Vi kommer till er
Pricing transparency
Quote-only sales rep contact required. AED purchase prices reportedly 10,000-18,000 SEK on knowledge-base content, no monthly figure published.
Trust signals
50+ years operating · nationwide branch network · "thousands of certified workplaces" · ISO 9001/14001 · corporate-customer logos (rare on homepage but cited in case-studies tab).
Distinguishing features
The only player bundling AED into a multi-service facility contract (fire + first aid + AED). Field-service technicians on the road already.
Pulse4all wedge
Presto's strength (broad facility-services contract) is its weakness for AED-only buyers — they don't want to negotiate a fire-extinguisher contract to get a defibrillator. Pulse4all's narrow, all-in €29.95 subscription is friction-free; cancel-anytime kills the multi-year facility-services lock-in.
#3 · Closest direct comp · 279 SEK/mo all-in

Hjärtstartarspecialisten

hjartstartarspecialisten.se
"Specialisten som hjälper dig att välja rätt."
What's bundled
Complete packages: AED + cabinet + electrodes + signage; "8 års garanti", free support. Standalone purchase from ~13,000-30,000 SEK; rental "from a few hundred kronor/month" with service agreement bundled (advertised value 2,500 SEK/year). Hyrespaket Large includes everything for monthly fee. Insurance + full service from 279 SEK/month (~€24).
Messaging themes
Specialist 8 års garanti Tryggt, prisvärt & smidigt Komplett paket Fullservice & garanti
Pricing transparency
Strong Published price points on category pages — one of the most transparent on the Swedish list.
Trust signals
"Bästa pris online" · case-study e-commerce reference · 8-year warranty as standard · phone/email support window stated (mon-fri 8-17).
Distinguishing features
Closest Swedish analogue to a productised subscription — published "from 279 SEK/mo" all-in. Pulse4all's most direct rival.
Pulse4all wedge
Hjärtstartarspecialisten is a rental contract (multi-year, locked), not a subscription. Pulse4all's cancel-anytime is the wedge. Also, 279 SEK ≈ €24/mo is device-only insurance/service; Pulse4all at ~€30 includes consumables refresh + theft/damage + training portal in one line.
#4 · Pan-European pure e-commerce · 90K AED claim

Medisol Hjärtstartshop

hjartstartshop.se
"Europas största sortiment av hjärtstartare och tillbehör."
What's bundled
Pure e-commerce: device + accessories + optional service contract via Medisol Service Center (function checks, shock test, software updates, electrode/battery replacement). Brand-independent — sells ZOLL, Philips, HeartSine, Schiller, Defibtech, DefiSign.
Messaging themes
Sedan 2004 Europas största lager AED inom 6 minuter senast 2030 Märkesoberoende Snabb leverans
Pricing transparency
High Typical e-commerce with prices on every SKU.
Trust signals
90,000+ AEDs delivered (group claim) · 20+ years · Medisol Service Center certification · Trustpilot reviews surfaced.
Distinguishing features
Largest pan-European inventory and the only player with depth across all major AED brands at published prices.
Pulse4all wedge
Medisol sells a transaction. Customer pays 13-25K SEK upfront, then has to remember to renew electrodes/battery in 2-4 years (high churn, high failure-to-fire risk). Pulse4all replaces the transaction with a relationship — €29.95/mo, consumables show up automatically, no calendar reminder needed.
#5 · Cleanest rental pricing · ZOLL AED Plus 394 SEK/mo

Brandfast

brandfast.se
"Köp eller hyr din hjärtstartare — vi har lösningen."
What's bundled
Buy or rent. Rental example: ZOLL AED 3 at 647 SEK/month ex-VAT (60-month contract, quarterly billing); ZOLL AED Plus at 394 SEK/month. Free support agreement on every unit. Optional service agreement at 169 SEK/month. Insurance: 250 SEK/year post-year-1 on purchases; included free for entire rental period when renting.
Messaging themes
Hyr eller köp Stöd ingår 60 månaders hyresavtal Försäkring ingår vid hyra Hjärtstartarpaket
Pricing transparency
Excellent Every SKU shows monthly rent, support price, insurance price.
Trust signals
Family-owned Swedish operator · pricing transparency itself is a trust signal in this market.
Distinguishing features
Most explicit rental pricing on the Swedish list · cleanest package structure (ZOLL AED 3 paket 14 etc.).
Pulse4all wedge
Brandfast's 60-month commitment is the wedge — Pulse4all is cancel-anytime. €29.95/mo all-in vs 394 + 169 + €0 = 563 SEK ≈ €49/mo at Brandfast's lowest tier (ZOLL AED Plus rent + service). Pulse4all is materially cheaper AND more flexible, on like-for-like ZOLL Plus.
#6 · Training-led B2B · 5K customers, 4.9/5

WorkSafe Sverige

worksafesverige.se
"Brandskydd | Utbildning | AED | Första Hjälpen — för en tryggare arbetsplats."
What's bundled
Training-led: 2-hour HLR/AED course (Swedish or English) on-site, modern manikins, AED hands-on, certificate. AED hardware secondary — sold via partner shop (worksafesverige.se/butik). Training is the lead, hardware is the upsell.
Messaging themes
5 000+ företag 4,9/5 betyg Sveriges mest uppskattade Vi kommer till er Sedan 2016
Pricing transparency
Quote-only on training packages · published prices on shop SKUs.
Trust signals
5,000+ corporate customers · 4.9/5 review rating headline · Sollentuna address.
Distinguishing features
Strong B2B training-first proposition — they convert training customers into hardware customers, not the other way round.
Pulse4all wedge
WorkSafe doesn't really compete on subscription AED — they compete on training contracts. Pulse4all's wedge: include the training (online portal, certificate) inside the €29.95/mo, and WorkSafe's "you also need us for the course" upsell collapses.
#7 · Owns the category-defining domain · 10K orgs claim

Hjärtstartare.se

hjartstartare.se
"Köp eller hyr din AED hos oss."
What's bundled
Buy or rent. Rental package wraps support, service, maintenance into the monthly fee. Models featured: LIFEPAK CR2, HeartSine Samaritan PAD. Group claim: "nearly 10,000 organisations supplied."
Messaging themes
Rädda liv 10 000 organisationer Marknadsledande modeller HLR-instruktörer godkända Komplett från en leverantör
Pricing transparency
Mixed Clear "12,000-25,000 SEK ex-VAT" buy range; rental is "låg månadsavgift" without a published number on top pages.
Trust signals
10,000-org claim · HLR Council-approved instructors · LIFEPAK / HeartSine partnership badges.
Distinguishing features
Owns the category-defining domain (hjartstartare.se is generic for "defibrillator" in Swedish) — strong organic SEO advantage.
Pulse4all wedge
Domain is the moat, not the product. Customer who lands on hjartstartare.se still has to call sales for the rental quote. Pulse4all wins on instant-checkout: pick plan → enter card → device dispatched. No phone, no quote, no PDF contract.
