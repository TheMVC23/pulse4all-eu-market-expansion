Germany

Live market · ~85-100 subs/mo · Largest absolute AED market in EU

Largest single AED market in Europe by absolute revenue (~€90-120M/yr, ~20-28K units/yr). Highly fragmented — no national chain owns more than ~10-15% market share. Berufsgenossenschaft (sector-specific employer insurance bodies) is the dominant B2B procurement channel. Since April 2015 every driver's-licence applicant has been demo'd an AED in the mandatory Erste-Hilfe-Kurs — baseline familiarity is the highest in Europe. Pulse4all wedges in on the B2C/Privatpersonen segment that incumbents have effectively conceded, plus flex-term rental where Kroschke matches us on price (€34.95) but locks 36 months.

Section 01 · Market Analysis

Largest, most fragmented, most BG-driven AED market in EU

Germany combines the EU's biggest absolute spend with the EU's most fragmented competitor field. No single national chain dominates. Berufsgenossenschaft channels (BGHM, BGW, BG ETEM, BGN) drive workplace AED demand via compliance recommendations + subsidy programmes. Made-in-Germany is a real procurement filter — PRIMEDIC/Schiller own it. Pulse4all (Philips-device, NL-headquartered) needs explicit "German service team + DE legal entity" trust-build to neutralise the patriotic-procurement angle.

Regulatory & structural drivers

1
DGUV Information 204-010
AED recommended at workplaces (not mandated)
Recommends AEDs as part of betriebliche Erste Hilfe. Companies treat it as de-facto compliance. DGUV Grundsatz 304-001 covers first-aider training including AED demo.
2
Berufsgenossenschaft channel
BGHM, BGW, BG ETEM, BGN — workplace pipeline
Sector-specific employer insurance bodies actively recommend AEDs + run subsidy/co-funding programmes. Compound distribution channel — winning a BG endorsement is worth more than any direct marketing spend.
3
Erste-Hilfe-Kurs reform (Apr 2015)
AED demo mandatory for driver's licence
9-hour first-aid course required for every German driver's licence applicant + every workplace first-aider. AED demonstration baked in. ~3 million Germans/yr now cycle through AED-aware training. Baseline familiarity = highest in EU.
4
Defikataster (Definetz e.V.)
De-facto national AED registry · 43K locations
No statutory backing, but the only national registry. Pulse4all should ensure every deployed unit is registered — free trust signal at zero CAC.
5
Björn-Steiger-Stiftung Herzsicher
Foundation-funded community AED programme
Steiger Stiftung funds AEDs for clubs/Kommunen. LSBs in Hessen, Rheinland-Pfalz, Bayern publish AED-funding lines. Partnership/co-marketing channel, not commercial competitor.
6
No federal AED mandate
Soft recommendation + insurer pressure
Unlike France (Décret 2018-1186) or Italy (2022 sports law). Regulation is BG + insurer-driven, not statutory. Slower-burn market — but absolute volume is already very high.

Section 02 · Competitive Landscape

11 named competitors · No 800-pound gorilla

Germany's competitive field is the most fragmented in Europe. WERO (rental, quote-gated) and Kroschke (rental, €34.95/mo published with 36-month lock-in) are the most-cited national distributors. PRIMEDIC and Schiller own "Made in Germany." MedX5 and AED.de win transparent ecommerce. Fröhlich Dienste is the closest commercial twin to Pulse4all at €29.99/mo + 1-month minimum — but sub-scale.

Strategic takeaways

1
Privatpersonen / B2C is empty
No incumbent targets households / Privatpersonen with a clean monthly proposition. Pulse4all's clearest wedge — and the highest-conviction expansion lever for the 85→200+ subs/mo ramp.
2
Kroschke matches our price · loses on lock-in
Kroschke published €34.95/mo (identical to Pulse4all HS1) but with 36-month minimum. "Cancel anytime · 3-month minimum" is the closing argument.
3
Made-in-Germany counter-narrative
PRIMEDIC owns "Made in Germany"; Pulse4all is Philips devices + NL HQ. Counter with: "DE service team + DE legal entity + Defikataster registration + Steiger-Stiftung partnership". Don't fight on Made-in-Germany; reframe trust.
4
Defikataster + Steiger Stiftung = zero-CAC trust
List every Pulse4all-deployed unit on Defikataster. Support Steiger Stiftung Herzsicher programmes for community goodwill. Both are zero-CAC ways to build the DE trust narrative.
5
PRIMEROS = partner not enemy
PRIMEROS owns the Erste-Hilfe-Kurs B2B channel + uses it for AED rental upsell. Pulse4all has no Schulungspartner status — partner with PRIMEROS-type training networks rather than fight for BG-anerkannte status from scratch.
6
Berufsgenossenschaft endorsement = compound distribution
BG-anerkannte status from one sector (BGHM metalworking, BGW healthcare, BGN hospitality, BG ETEM energy/telco) unlocks compound B2B distribution. Investment-grade play for 2026-2027.
