Belgium · Top 7 competitor deep-dive

CardioService · CardioPro BE · AED-Partner · Defibrion BE · AEDwinkel · Medisol · CardiAid Benelux

Each competitor profiled across 7 dimensions plus the cross-competitor messaging matrix. Key Belgian dynamics: dual-language (NL/FR), two volume incumbents (CardioService 15K installs, AED-Partner 50K Benelux deliveries), rental band €33-39 HTVA with startup-fee + multi-year-lock-in pattern.

Country snapshot · 60-second read
Belgium · top-line
Purchase-dominant · two volume incumbents · €33-39 HTVA rental band with startup fees and 5-7 yr lock-ins
Belgium is purchase-dominant with rental as the emerging tier. Two volume claimants — CardioService (15,000 installs) and AED-Partner (50,000 Benelux deliveries) — anchor the B2B incumbency story. Rental headlines cluster at €33-€39/mo HTVA (CardiAid €33, CardioService €39, CardioPro BE €39) but most rivals attach a startup fee (€249 at CardioService) or 5-7-year lock-in. Hardware-first players (Defibrion, Medisol, AEDwinkel) compete on warranty and SKU breadth — Medisol's 10-year warranty is the standout. The gap: TTC, no-startup, no-commitment, particulier-friendly rental. Pulse4all's €29.95-34.95 TTC, cancel-anytime offer is structurally cleaner than every Belgian rental quoted HTVA with a startup fee or multi-year contract.

Top 7 — Belgium

#1 · Volume incumbent · 15K+ installs

CardioService

cardioservice.eu
"Koop of huur een AED toestel, aangepast aan jouw omgeving" — "AED specialist nr. 1 in België."
What's bundled
Rental: €249 startup fee + €39/mo HT = device + all-in maintenance contract (5 or 7 years) + installation + insurance + training. Purchase tier €1,200-€2,500 device-only (maintenance/training à la carte).
Messaging themes
Specialist nr. 1 15 000+ AED installaties Installatie · onderhoud · verzekering & opleiding Gratis maatwerk-offerte Top merken
Pricing transparency
Medium €39/mo + €249 startup is published but locked into 5-/7-year contracts; purchase range disclosed.
Trust signals
"15 000 AED installaties" (largest install base claim in BE) · 7+ brand-partner logos · LinkedIn corporate page · bilingual NL/FR site.
Distinguishing features
Largest deployed base in Belgium · insurance is bundled (rare) · multi-brand offer (the buyer chooses) · dedicated sub-pages for each AED brand.
Pulse4all wedge
CardioService's €249 startup + 5-7-year lock-in is the antithesis of Pulse4all. No startup fee, cancel anytime, €29.95-34.95 TTC, AED at the door in 48 h, no contract negotiation. Where CardioService wins on incumbency, Pulse4all wins on friction-removal.
#2 · Direct subscription competitor · €39 HTVA

CardioPro Belgium

cardiopro.be
"Défibrillateur Belgique — Vente & Location DAE."
What's bundled
Rental from €39/mo HTVA = certified CE device + delivery 48 h + training included + reactive maintenance across BE. Phone-line 7/7. Dedicated "conseiller dédié" from order to install.
Messaging themes
À partir de 39 €/mois HTVA Livraison sous 48h Formation incluse Garantie 8 ans Conforme aux normes belges et européennes Conseiller dédié
Pricing transparency
High €39/mo HTVA published.
Trust signals
8-year warranty on hardware · CE certification call-out · 7/7 phone support · FR HQ extension into BE (cardiopro.fr group) · +32 466 90 13 49 BE number.
Distinguishing features
Cross-border FR-BE structure with French-led pricing discipline · explicit 8-year hardware guarantee (longer than most peers).
Pulse4all wedge
CardioPro BE is €39 HTVA = €47.20 TTC — 40%+ above Pulse4all's €34.95 TTC headline. Pulse4all wins on TTC clarity and on the lack of a "conseiller dédié" gauntlet — most particulier and SME buyers want self-serve, not a sales rep.
#3 · Volume incumbent · 50K Benelux deliveries

AED-Partner

aedpartner.be
"Dé totaalleverancier van AED's in de Benelux" / "Number 1 total supplier of AEDs in Benelux."
What's bundled
Two service tiers — Plus and All-in maintenance contracts (separate from device sale). 6-hour on-site SLA after deployment use. Device sales €489-€2,000+ depending on brand. Training and cabinet quoted as line items.
Messaging themes
Sinds 2003 50 000+ AED's geleverd Hartveilige samenleving Plus of All-in onderhoudscontract Binnen 6 uur ter plaatse na inzet Grootste assortiment
Pricing transparency
Medium-high Full e-commerce catalog with €489 entry · service contracts quote-driven.
Trust signals
50 000 AEDs delivered (largest claim of any Benelux player) · 9+ brand logos (Cardiac Science, Zoll, Mindray, Philips, Physio-Control, Defibtech, HeartSine, Primedic, Schiller) · 22+ years of operation · .be + .com + .nl footprint.
Distinguishing features
Largest install base in Benelux · the 6-hour post-use intervention SLA is unique · B2B/wholesaler-leaning (broader range, deeper SKU catalog).
Pulse4all wedge
AED-Partner is purchase-led — the customer still ends up with a €1,500 cheque + a service contract on top. Pulse4all reframes the same outcome as €29-34/mo TTC, no purchase decision needed, no service-contract negotiation. AED-Partner serves the hartstichting / corporate procurement buyer; Pulse4all owns the long tail.
#4 · Hardware specialist · exclusive HeartSine importer

Defibrion BE

defibrion.com/be
"De specialist in AED's en veiligheidsmaterialen" / "Oplossingen voor veiligheid in jouw omgeving."
What's bundled
Sale-led. Entry AED package from €1,189 (e.g. LIFEPAK CR2 incl. ARKY indoor cabinet + 5 AED stickers + Resuscitation Kit). Maintenance contract is separate, paid annually. HeartSine exclusive Benelux + France importer.
Messaging themes
Specialist Exclusieve importeur HeartSine Levert · installeert · onderhoudt AED moet altijd werken
Pricing transparency
High Full e-commerce, €1,189 entry openly listed.
Trust signals
Exclusive HeartSine importer status · transparent webshop with package pricing · multi-country site (NL/BE/FR).
Distinguishing features
Exclusive HeartSine importer for Benelux + France — a hardware moat. Bundled-package SKUs (device + cabinet + stickers + kit) lower the cognitive load vs pure-line catalog.
Pulse4all wedge
Defibrion's bundle still asks for €1,189 capex up-front + recurring service. Pulse4all converts the same outcome into €29.95-34.95/mo opex with cabinet, signs, electrodes, monitoring, replacement device all included. Defibrion wins the buyer who has CFO sign-off; Pulse4all wins everyone else.

aedwinkel.be
"AED toestel kopen? Advies van een specialist."
What's bundled
Pure e-commerce — device + accessories sold à la carte. Maintenance contract optional. Cabinets, electrodes, batteries listed as separate SKUs. Specialist phone-advice positioning.
Messaging themes
Advies van een specialist AED kopen Snelle levering Product-led
Pricing transparency
Very high Full e-commerce price grid.
Trust signals
".be specialist" positioning · advice-led copy · broad SKU range.
Distinguishing features
Cleanest pure-Belgian e-commerce experience — every device, accessory, electrode, sticker has a SKU and a price.
Pulse4all wedge
AEDwinkel sells transactions, not relationships. After the box arrives, the customer is on their own for compliance, electrode tracking, battery expiry and incident response. Pulse4all bundles the entire post-purchase compliance burden into the €29.95-34.95/mo subscription — turning a one-time sale into recurring peace-of-mind.
#6 · International parent · 10-year warranty

Medisol / Defibrillateurshop BE

defibrillateurshop.be
"Medisol — Défibrillateur, formation, réanimation."
What's bundled
Sale-led with rich add-on options. Hardware €999 (Philips HS1) → €2,149 (DefiSign Pocket Plus). 10-year warranty on all AEDs, free delivery, optional annual maintenance & service contract, free electrode + battery replacement after a deployment (rare benefit). Training programs sold separately.
Messaging themes
Spécialiste défibrillateurs Garantie 10 ans Livraison gratuite Remplacement gratuit après usage Mission : sauver des vies
Pricing transparency
Very high Explicit per-model price list (Zoll AED Plus €1,259, Philips FRx €1,345, HS1 €999).
Trust signals
International parent (Medisol International — multi-country) · 10-year warranty (longest on this list) · reseller B2B program (sells to installers) · Medisol Service Center sub-brand for maintenance.
Distinguishing features
10-year hardware warranty is the strongest hardware promise in the FR/BE set · free post-deployment consumables · serves both end-customers AND resellers (B2B2B layer).
Pulse4all wedge
Medisol is transactional and brand-agnostic — they win the price-shopper. Pulse4all wins the "I-don't-want-to-think-about-this-again" buyer with a single monthly sticker that includes the same post-deployment consumable replacement Medisol charges separately for outside the contract.
#7 · Direct subscription competitor · €33/mo single-brand

CardiAid Benelux

cardiaid.nl
"CardiAid — de meest gebruiksvriendelijke AED" / "The most user-friendly AED on the market."
What's bundled
Two clear paths. Purchase: fully automatic AED from €1,479. Rental: from €33/mo (semi- or fully-automatic) — or "from €1/day" headline. Both paths include maintenance.
Messaging themes
Meest gebruiksvriendelijk Levens redden Officiële importeur AED vanaf €1/dag Particulieren én bedrijven
Pricing transparency
High Purchase entry €1,479 and rental €33/mo both published · €1/day pricing reframe.
Trust signals
Official Benelux distributor for CardiAid brand · LinkedIn corporate footprint · particulier-friendly rental option (rare in the BE set) · bilingual NL/BE site.
Distinguishing features
Single-brand depth (CardiAid only) → strong UX/usability narrative · only Benelux player explicitly courting particuliers with rental (€1/day framing) · rental-included maintenance.
Pulse4all wedge
CardiAid Benelux is €33/mo for one specific brand — a strong direct comp. Pulse4all matches the price (€29.95-34.95) but is brand-agnostic, ships to NL/UK/DE/FR/SE today, and is built for online subscription conversion (one-page checkout) rather than CardiAid's dealer-style site flow.
