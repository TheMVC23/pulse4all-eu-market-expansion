Country snapshot · 60-second read
Norway · top-line
Charity (LHL/Røde Kors) holds trust · Folkestarteren is only subscription native, on 24-60 month tie-ins
Norway's AED market splits cleanly along a charity vs commercial fault line. Røde Kors and LHL hold the trust high-ground via patient-association and humanitarian heritage — they own training and brand legitimacy. The commercial side is led by Mediq/NorAid (institutional, public-sector tenders) and a long tail of price-led sellers (1Aid, Hjertestarteren) who explicitly attack subscriptions with "no service needed, 8-year warranty" messaging. Folkestarteren is the only true subscription native, but locks customers into 24-60 month tie-ins. Pulse4all wedge: 1-month rolling subscription, all-inclusive at €29.95, with 24/7 monitoring no Norwegian competitor offers — undercutting Folkestarteren on flexibility, beating 1Aid on cash flow, and complementing (not fighting) LHL/Røde Kors training.

Top 7 — Norway

#1 · Only true subscription native · 60-month tie-ins

Folkestarteren (Osnordic AS)

www.folkestarteren.no
"Folkestarteren tilbyr lavkostnads leie av hjertestartere" — low-cost rental of defibrillators.
What's bundled
Monthly rental of CardiAid or Mindray AED, free CPR + AED training (on-site or online), regular service, machine replacement if used, customer service 5 days a week, battery and electrode replacements when needed.
Messaging themes
Lavkostnad Leie i stedet for kjøp Gratis kurs Tilgjengelig for små bedrifter og enkeltpersoner Vi jobber for flere hjertestartere i Norge
Pricing transparency
Fully published 490 NOK/mo on a 60-month tie-in (≈ €43); 590 NOK/mo on 36 months (≈ €51); 790 NOK/mo on 24 months (≈ €69) plus a 1,990 NOK setup fee. Three transparent tiers.
Trust signals
Owned by Osnordic AS, Oslo HQ · Listed on karrierestart.no as employer · CardiAid and Mindray as device brand partners.
Distinguishing features
Cheapest published all-in subscription in Norway and the only Norwegian player who runs a 'rent, never sell' model on the homepage. Brand name 'Folkestarteren' deliberately echoes 'AED for the people.'
Pulse4all wedge
Pulse4all matches the rent-not-sell logic but undercuts the tie-in: 1-month rolling at €29.95 vs Folkestarteren's 60-month tie-in to hit their lowest rate. Pulse4all also bundles 24/7 monitoring and CPR-trained operator support, which Folkestarteren does not offer.
#2 · Anti-subscription pitch · 8-year warranty hook

Medix Førstehjelp

www.medix.no
"Medix Førstehjelp AS" — full-service first-aid supplier.
What's bundled
Device sale (Lifeline AED, Zoll AED Plus), course, service agreement, leasing/financing, accessories. Lifeline pitched as '8-year warranty, no service required, replacement device if it fails.'
Messaging themes
Norsk veiledning 8 års garanti — ingen service nødvendig IP55 vann- og støvbeskyttelse Brukervennlig Kurs, service og finansiering — alt under ett tak
Pricing transparency
Mixed Device prices published per product. Service and lease prices quote-only on most pages.
Trust signals
Brand partners (Defibtech, Zoll) · Norwegian-language NRR-aligned voice prompts · Long-running first-aid retailer.
Distinguishing features
Pushes 'no service contract needed' angle on Lifeline — anti-subscription positioning, the inverse of Pulse4all.
Pulse4all wedge
Medix's 'buy outright, no service' pitch leaves customers responsible for tracking pad/battery expiry themselves. Pulse4all's all-inclusive monthly removes that admin burden and the 12-18k NOK upfront cost. Frame: 'Why pay 16,000 NOK upfront and still manage expiry yourself, when €30/mo covers everything?'
#3 · 52K members · Norwegian Heart and Lung Association

LHL Førstehjelp

forstehjelp.lhl.no
"LHL Førstehjelp — størst på førstehjelpskurs" — backed by Landsforeningen for Hjerte- og Lungesyke.
What's bundled
Devices (Cardiolife AED-3100, Lifeline AED with 7-yr battery), courses (Hjertestarterkurs, Kombi førstehjelp + AED, refresher), corporate-tailored training, accessories.
Messaging themes
NRR-sertifiserte instruktører Følger Norsk Førstehjelpsråd-standarden Tilpasser kurs til bedrifter Hjertestans-ekspertise Vi redder liv
Pricing transparency
Mixed Devices priced per page · courses are 'request a quote'.
Trust signals
Charity backing — LHL is one of Norway's largest patient associations. NRR (Norsk Resuscitasjonsråd) certification. Legitimacy advantage no commercial reseller can replicate.
Distinguishing features
Mission-driven brand. Course portfolio tightly tied to Norwegian resuscitation council standards.
Pulse4all wedge
LHL's strength is courses, not device aftercare. Pulse4all positions as the operational layer underneath — 'keep your LHL training, let us handle the device, pads and battery management.' Co-existence pitch, not head-on attack.
#4 · 100+ years · institutional distributor · SWAP service

Mediq Norge / NorAid

www.mediqnorge.no
"Din ledende leverandør av medisinsk utstyr" — Norway's leading medical-supplies distributor, 100+ years.
What's bundled
Wide AED range (Defibtech Lifeline, Lifeline VIEW), NorAid-branded first-aid kits, SWAP service with loaner unit, batteries and pads included on contract. Free shipping over 1,500 NOK.
Messaging themes
100 år med leveranser til norsk helsevesen SWAP-service — vi sender lånestarter Eget merke NorAid Bredt utvalg fra flere leverandører Tilfredsstiller nasjonale krav
Pricing transparency
Service-only published Service avtale 'fra kun kr. 390 per måned' (≈ €34/mo) — but service-only on customer-owned device. Device pricing on product pages.
Trust signals
Mediq Group (Dutch-Nordic, multi-billion-euro distributor) · Public-sector contracts · Norwegian healthcare for >100 years.
Distinguishing features
Only player with the SWAP-and-return service-by-mail model. Owns NorAid as private label.
Pulse4all wedge
Mediq is built for hospitals and public tenders, not SMEs. Their 390 NOK service fee assumes the customer already bought a 15-18K NOK device. Pulse4all's €29.95 wraps device + service in one — better TCO over 36 months and zero capex.
#5 · The Red Cross · unmatched humanitarian brand trust

Røde Kors Førstehjelp

www.rodekorsforstehjelp.no
"Størst på førstehjelp i Norge" — Norway's largest in first-aid courses and AEDs.
What's bundled
AEDs, first-aid courses (4-hr with AED focus, 3-hr with AED demo, 5-hr basic, 5-hr psychosocial), corporate courses, equipment.
Messaging themes
Størst på førstehjelp i Norge Landets største nettverk av sertifiserte instruktører Lokalt forankret over hele landet Vi redder liv Røde Kors-kvalitet
Pricing transparency
Mixed Quote-driven for corporate · courses publicly priced per session.
Trust signals
The Norwegian Red Cross — strongest possible humanitarian-brand trust signal in Norway. Nationwide instructor network. Charity status.
Distinguishing features
Brand only — no commercial reseller can match Red Cross legitimacy. Unmatched local instructor footprint.
Pulse4all wedge
Red Cross courses are a one-off purchase; the device still needs lifecycle care. Pulse4all sits underneath — 'we keep the AED you bought via Red Cross alive and compliant.' Not a battle for share of voice; battle for share of monthly wallet.
#6 · Most explicit anti-subscription pitch · 8yr warranty

1Aid Norge / Medical Helse

www.medicalhelse.no
"1Aid Norge — ledende aktør innen førstehjelp og beredskap."
What's bundled
Defibtech Lifeline AED (15,399 NOK / ≈ €1,340), Lifeline View ML (18,990 NOK / ≈ €1,650), pads at 725 NOK, training, equipment packages.
Messaging themes
Ingen kostbare serviceavtaler 8 års garanti — får ny maskin om noe skjer Instruktører fra ambulanse, brannvesen og politi Markedets mest prisgunstige enhet Skreddersydde kurs
Pricing transparency
Strong on hardware Device and pad prices fully published. No subscription product.
Trust signals
Instructors recruited from ambulance, fire and police · Defibtech as flagship brand · Long-running shop.
Distinguishing features
Most explicit anti-subscription pitch in Norway — sells 8-year warranty as the 'no service needed' alternative.
Pulse4all wedge
1Aid's customer pays 15,399 NOK day-one and still has to track pads (2-yr) and battery (5-yr) themselves. Over 36 months Pulse4all costs ~12,420 NOK total, zero capex, with pads and battery shipped automatically and 24/7 monitoring. We win on cash flow and on convenience.
#7 · Discount/clearance angle · 'tilbudstorget'

Hjertestarteren.no

www.hjertestarteren.no
"Komplette hjertestarterpakker for privat og bedrift."
What's bundled
Full device pakker (AED + cabinet + signage + pads + battery), service agreements (Hjertevakten badge: komplett and pluss tiers), tilbudstorget (offers section), backorder package pricing.
Messaging themes
Tilbudstorget — best pris på lager Komplette pakker Serviceavtale for bedrifter Kontroll av hjertestarter Hurtig levering
Pricing transparency
Mixed Device package pricing visible in offers section · service agreements quote-driven via Hjertevakten partnership.
Trust signals
Norsk Hjertestarterregister registration · Hjertevakten partnership · Stock-ready offer model.
Distinguishing features
Discount/clearance angle ('tilbudstorget') — the bargain-hunter's AED shop.
Pulse4all wedge
Customers shopping the discount aisle are price-sensitive and feeling the upfront cost. Pulse4all converts that pain into a €30/mo opex line — same psychological lever, better cash-flow story.
