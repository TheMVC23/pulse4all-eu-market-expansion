Spain

Regional GTM (Catalonia, Madrid, Andalucía first) outperforms a national approach.

Section 01 · Market Analysis

Top-5 EU market · 17-community regulatory patchwork

A 2023 Journal of Healthcare Quality Research study (Elsevier) found rates ranging 35-126 DEAs per 100,000 depending on regional legislation. Communities with mandatory installation laws averaged 92.1/100k; those without 57.8/100k. Madrid leads, Andalucía 0.23/1,000. Total installed base estimate 35,000-50,000 AEDs. ~7-8% YoY growth driven by Catalonia, Madrid, Andalucía, Valencia legislative pushes.

Regulatory drivers — three layers

1
Real Decreto 365/2009
National framework
Sets minimum safety/quality conditions for AED use outside healthcare settings (BOE 2009). National baseline; everything above this is regional.
2
17 autonomous-community decrees
Regional cardioprotection laws
Andalucía Decreto 22/2012, Madrid Decreto 78/2017, Cataluña Decret 151/2012, etc. Installation obligations vary by region — some mandatory, some advisory. Drives the 35-126/100k density spread.
3
AEMPS
EU MDR competent authority
Agencia Española de Medicamentos y Productos Sanitarios — competent authority for AED medical-device oversight under EU MDR. Standard pathway.

National foundations

Heart foundation
Fundación Española del Corazón (FEC)
Patient education, cardioprotection advocacy. Affiliated with Sociedad Española de Cardiología. Key partnership target for credibility. fundaciondelcorazon.com
Information hub
Cardioprotegidos & Salvavidas Cardio
Industry information sites tracking regional legislation: cardioprotegidos.es/legislacion-ccaa · salvavidas.com/normativa. Critical for compliance work per region.
Channel into Catalonia
Generalitat + Decret 151/2012
Catalonia is the most-legislated region with strongest mandate. Recommended first-region focus for Spanish launch — clearest demand + highest density.

Section 02 · Competitive Landscape

Crowded subscription market · Proyecto Salvavidas dominates

Subscription model presence: VERY HIGH. Spain has the most developed AED renting market among the priority countries. Regional regulations drive demand. The market has 10+ active rental providers. Pricing (€45-80/mo) is above Pulse4all's NL pricing — Pulse4all enters at the floor. Local regulatory knowledge per region is essential. Spanish-language CC mandatory.

Key takeaways

1
Proyecto Salvavidas is the gorilla
17,000+ protected entities. Owns the institutional + cardioprotección compliance brand. Pulse4all attacks SME + sports clubs, not their stronghold.
2
Regional GTM beats national
17 communities with different rules. Catalonia, Madrid, Andalucía, Valencia are the legislated entry points. Sequential regional rollout outperforms a national push from day 1.
3
Spanish CC is non-negotiable
Price-sensitive market. €33/mo positioning will face friction without Spanish phone rescue. CC adds €5K/mo agent cost — already in the operational plan.
4
Latin America satellite play
Cracking Spain proves the model for the much larger Spanish-speaking opportunity (Mexico, Colombia, Argentina, Chile). Long-term satellite multiplier on the Spanish localisation investment.
