Country snapshot · 60-second read
Spain · top-line
Renting/cardioprotección dominant · €35-80/mo cluster · Pulse4all undercuts by 30-60% with online activation
Spain's AED market is dominated by renting/cardioprotección integral language — almost every player wraps device, installation, mantenimiento, formación and señalización into a monthly fee, with prices clustering at €35-€80/month. The category leader, Salvavidas Cardio (acquired by Sweden's Safe Life in July 2025), uses scale-claims (17,000+ protected entities, 19,000+ rescue points) and partnerships with prevention societies (SEMICYUC, AEEMT) as moats. Regional and city-level resellers (Fyamedical, Medilate, Cardioalquiler) compete on flexibility and SEMICYUC certification. The Spanish market is highly regulation-led — every reseller leans on "comunidad autónoma compliance" rather than UX or speed. Pulse4all wedge: digital-native, sub-€35 price, online activation, no broker layer.

Top 7 — Spain

#1 · Category leader · 17,000+ entities · Safe Life (SE) acquired July 2025

Proyecto Salvavidas / Salvavidas Cardio

www.salvavidas.com
"Servicios integrales de cardioprotección" / "La principal iniciativa de cardioprotección en España."
What's bundled
DEA de última generación, instalación, mantenimiento preventivo y correctivo, formación online en SVB, conexión 24/7 a 112/061, gestión administrativa y registro, certificación, señalización, carcasa exterior, plan de difusión RSC.
Messaging themes
más de 17,000 entidades protegidas 19,000 puntos de rescate cardíaco principal iniciativa en España sin contratos de permanencia Cardio-as-a-Service
Pricing transparency
Quote-only Quote-only on homepage; three tiers (básico, estándar, Cardioprotect). No price visible without contact form.
Trust signals
Acquired by Safe Life Group (Sweden, July 2025), AEEMT partner, Adif partnership, ROSP certification, accredited training in all comunidades autónomas, public-sector co-branding.
Distinguishing features
Only player with nationwide >10,000 installed base claim · in-house solidarity programmes (pulsera Salvavidas, cardiovending) · framework agreements with prevention mutuas.
Pulse4all wedge
Their quote-only, rep-driven funnel is slow and opaque; Pulse4all's transparent €29.95-€34.95 subscription and online activation cut sales cycle from weeks to minutes. Attack their SME and gym segments where the Cardio-as-a-Service narrative is overkill.

Distinguishing features
Strong sport-centre vertical (gimnasios, polideportivos) · Nationwide coverage including islands · Hemorrhage-control training (rare differentiator).
Pulse4all wedge
At 49€/mo + IVA Proyecto Respira sits ~70% above Pulse4all's €29.95. Same all-inclusive promise at materially lower price; emphasise online checkout vs their phone-quote workflow.

Equipo + instalación + mantenimiento + formación homologada RCP/DESA + supervisión continua. Renting or purchase. Reciclaje formación cada 2 años (algunas CCAA anual).

Pulse4all wedge
Bottom of their range (€45) is still 30-50% above Pulse4all. Out-execute their content with English/multi-language guides; their €45+ floor is a clean price wedge.
