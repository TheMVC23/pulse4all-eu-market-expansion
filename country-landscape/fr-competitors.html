Country snapshot · 60-second read
France · top-line
Rental-dominated SME tier · €29 HT floor · €54 premium anchor · "Made in France" owned by Lifeaz
The French market is rental-dominated at the SME tier, with €29/mo HT (CardioPro / Heart Protekt) acting as a price floor and €54/mo (Coeurgence / Locacoeur) as the "premium full-service" anchor. Lifeaz owns "Made in France" and is the only player with a true product brand; everyone else competes as a service. Compliance language ("Class IIIB", "ERC", "obligation 2026") is heavily worked because of the 2018-2022 ERP defibrillator-mandate cycle. The big gap: transparent particulier-friendly TTC pricing. Most rivals quote HT, hide the rental tier, or force a devis. Pulse4all's €29.95 TTC, no-quote, no-commitment proposition slices straight through this thicket.

Top 7 — France

#1 · Direct B2C threat · Made-in-France brand

Lifeaz

lifeaz.co
"Lifeaz — Le défibrillateur français pensé pour tous."
What's bundled
Two products: (a) Outright purchase of the Clark AED at €990 — device-only, with optional Pack Sérénité add-on at €58.80/year (or €235.20/5 years) covering remote monitoring + electrode/battery renewal; (b) Monthly rental option (price gated behind quote/configurator). Cabinet, training and signage are add-ons, not bundled.
Messaging themes
Fabriqué en France Le plus abordable du marché Hyper simple Connecté 20K équipées 70 vies sauvées
Pricing transparency
High €990 device, €58.80/yr Pack Sérénité, /prices/individuals + /prices/small-businesses pages with line-item pricing. Rental price is less prominent (configurator-driven).
Trust signals
"20 000 familles et entreprises équipées" · "70 vies sauvées" · CE medical certification · manufacturing in Normandy · sold via Fédération Française de Tennis, Boulanger 150+ stores, GACD distribution.
Distinguishing features
Only French-manufactured AED on this list · built-in connectivity with self-test reporting · single-button compact form factor (21×21×7 cm, 1.3 kg) · D2C brand presence (consumer retail channels).
Pulse4all wedge
Lifeaz upsells maintenance as an annual extra on top of €990 hardware — a CFO-style buyer must approve €990 capex + ongoing renewals. Pulse4all reframes the same outcome as €29-34/mo opex with cabinet, electrodes, battery and replacement device all rolled in, cancel anytime. No €990 cheque, no upsell sequence.
#2 · Direct subscription competitor · €29 HT price-twin

CardioPro

cardiopro.fr
"Spécialiste du Défibrillateur Automatique et Semi Automatique" — "Ensemble, faisons battre les cœurs plus longtemps."
What's bundled
Rental package €29 HT/month = device + maintenance + consumables (electrodes/battery before expiry) + delivery in 48 h. Training distance included. Cabinet bundled in most ERP/business packs. Purchase tier (€990-€2,095 HT) is device-only with optional add-ons.
Messaging themes
Location dès 29€/mois Livraison sous 48H Formation à distance incluse Devis dans la journée Spécialiste pour les professionnels
Pricing transparency
Very high Entry rental €29 HT/mo, purchase range (€990-€2,095 HT), short-term rental (€119-€179 HT/mo) all on indexed landing pages. Quote-based for tailored bundles.
Trust signals
Founded 2017 by Anthony Monaldi · Paris HQ (88 av. des Ternes, 75017) · MedicalBusiness schema · areas served FR/BE/LU/CH · FAQ schema for SEO authority · Crisp live-chat.
Distinguishing features
Best published price on French market at €29 HT/mo · aggressive SEO landing-page footprint covering every long-tail (Paris, particulier, gilet, S-ICD) · 48 h commercial response.
Pulse4all wedge
CardioPro's €29 is HT (ex-VAT) — €34.80 TTC — and locks customers into 1-60-month formulas. Pulse4all is €29.95 TTC all-in, cancel anytime, no commitment ladder. Pulse4all also avoids CardioPro's Paris-centric, ERP-heavy positioning by speaking equally to particuliers and SMEs.
#3 · Premium full-service · single-brand depth

Coeurgence

coeurgence.com
"Location de défibrillateur de qualité : 54€/mois, maintenance incluse."
What's bundled
All-inclusive monthly rental at €54/mo = Cardiac Science Powerheart G5 device + installation + preventive & corrective maintenance + on-site visits + electrode/battery renewal at expiry/recall + remote monitoring + consumables management. Cabinet & training quoted separately.
Messaging themes
100% efficacité clinique Powerheart — fournisseur U.S. Marines Techniciens certifiés Sans engagement Tout-inclus
Pricing transparency
High €54/mo headline, no commitment.
Trust signals
Cardiac Science factory certification for technicians · U.S. Marines reference (military-grade trust play) · on-site servicing (not mail-back) · clinical-efficacy claim.
Distinguishing features
Single-device focus (Cardiac Science Powerheart G5) — depth over breadth · on-site technician visits as standard rather than postal swap · military / clinical credibility framing.
Pulse4all wedge
Coeurgence is €54/mo single brand, France-only Cardiac Science. Pulse4all is €29.95-34.95/mo — c. 35-45% cheaper at the headline — and brand-agnostic with stronger consumer/SME appeal. Coeurgence's "U.S. Marines" angle is enterprise-narrative; Pulse4all's "20-second order, AED at your door" is conversion-narrative.
#4 · Premium B2B-only · price hidden behind quote

Sauvie

sauvie.fr
"Défibrillateur cardiaque automatique pour professionnels."
What's bundled
Rental = connected device + installation + training + follow-up + maintenance + consumable replacement. "Tout est inclus, sans frais cachés." 48-h on-site intervention on tech alert at no extra cost. Cabinet typically included for ERP installs.
Messaging themes
Tout inclus, sans frais cachés Tarifs comparables à un abonnement téléphonique Défibrillateur connecté Pour professionnels, collectivités, associations Flexibilité
Pricing transparency
Medium Copy explicitly compares to phone-bill pricing but specific monthly figure is quote-only (depends on duration + model). No €/month displayed on homepage.
Trust signals
Trustindex widget (Google reviews) · Yoast SEO · quote-pages for collectivités/associations · B2B-only positioning.
Distinguishing features
Connected/remote-monitored device with 48-h SLA on tech alerts · explicit positioning of training as a service line (not just an add-on).
Pulse4all wedge
Sauvie hides the price behind a devis — the #1 conversion killer for SMEs and particuliers. Pulse4all publishes €29.95/mo on the homepage and converts in a single checkout — same "tout-inclus" promise, zero quote friction, plus particulier-friendly messaging Sauvie deliberately avoids.
#5 · Regional FR anchor · entry €29/mo HT but bundle creep

Heart Protekt

heart-protekt.fr
"Défibrillateur Bordeaux — Vente, Location, Maintenance."
What's bundled
Rental from €29/month (entry) up to €40-€70/mo for higher-end models = device + automatic consumables replacement + technical verifications + 24/7 hotline + preventive maintenance by certified technicians + first-aid training. Class IIIB CE certified.
Messaging themes
8 ans d'expérience Astreinte 24/7 Techniciens certifiés Classe IIIB Bordeaux et toute la France
Pricing transparency
High €29/mo entry advertised · range €40-€70 displayed · purchase €1,200-€2,000.
Trust signals
8-year tenure · Bordeaux regional anchor · HelloPro listing · Class IIIB certification call-out · 24/7 on-call.
Distinguishing features
Regional (Bordeaux) anchor + nationwide service — feels local even when delivering nationally · Class IIIB regulatory framing as a differentiator vs older Class IIb stock.
Pulse4all wedge
Heart Protekt's €29/mo entry is HT and entry-spec only — bundle creep pushes most customers to €40-€70/mo. Pulse4all's €29.95-34.95/mo is the all-in TTC sticker, no upsell ladder, and the same 24/7 promise without geographic anchoring that signals "small regional player."
#6 · "3-minute window" specialist · ERC compliance angle

Locacoeur

locacoeur.com
"Le spécialiste des 3 premières minutes de l'urgence."
What's bundled
Rental from €54/month = Cardiac Science / HeartSine GAD+® device + 24/7 remote monitoring + annual on-site maintenance by certified technicians + 48-h on-site intervention on malfunction/use + consumable replacement before expiry/post-use + budget-controlled (consumables in price).
Messaging themes
Les 3 premières minutes Conformité ERC Télésurveillance 24/7 Maintenance continue Capteur intelligent
Pricing transparency
Medium-high €54/mo published · quote required for multi-site / volume.
Trust signals
ERC (European Resuscitation Council) compliance claim · Cardiac Science partner · smart-sensor capability angle · juridical-coherence FAQ explaining rental legality (lawyer-grade content).
Distinguishing features
Real-time CPR-quality feedback (compression-depth sensor) as a demoable feature · legal-framework content (juridical-coherence FAQ) targeting compliance officers.
Pulse4all wedge
Locacoeur is €54/mo, 80% above Pulse4all's headline. Locacoeur's "3-minute window" narrative is excellent — Pulse4all can borrow the urgency framing while undercutting on price by ~€20/month and removing the "spécialiste" tone that scares away non-experts.
#7 · Training-led · "40,000 personnes formées"

CardioSecours

cardiosecours.fr
"Spécialiste de la vente, formation et suivi de défibrillateurs."
What's bundled
Sale-led, not rental-led. Packs (device + cabinet + signage + electrodes + carry-bag) priced from €594-€790+ for accessories/signs and €1,640+ HT for ZOLL devices. Training is a separate paid line. Maintenance via paid contract, not bundled monthly.
Messaging themes
Partenaire Cardiac Science · Philips · Zoll 40 000 personnes formées Conseil expert Hotline 04 97 02 24 10 Présent dans toute la France
Pricing transparency
High on hardware Full e-commerce catalog with €/HT prices · low on rental (no rental offer prominent).
Trust signals
"40 000 personnes formées" (massive training claim) · 3 brand-partner logos (Cardiac Science, Philips, Zoll) · operational hotline · full e-commerce stack (PrestaShop).
Distinguishing features
Training-led brand — 40,000 trained is the headline · multi-brand catalog vs single-brand competitors · pure e-commerce UX (cart, accessories, electrodes).
Pulse4all wedge
CardioSecours forces the customer to price out 6+ line items (device + cabinet + signs + pads + training + maintenance contract). Pulse4all bundles all six into one €29.95 line. CardioSecours wins enterprise training contracts; Pulse4all wins the SME and particulier who refuses to deal with à-la-carte friction.
