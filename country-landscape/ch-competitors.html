Country snapshot · 60-second read
Switzerland · top-line
No competitor publishes flat consumer-friendly monthly · Pulse4all enters as the first transparent online AED-as-a-Service in CH
Switzerland's AED market is small, premium-priced, and dominated by medical-professional credibility signals (SRC certification, ZOLL elite-partner status, emergency-physician-led teams). Average AED prices CHF 1,800-2,000 + VAT; rental subscriptions exist but are quote-driven and bundled with maintenance contracts. Procamed (aed.ch) is the dominant Swiss-wide player with the ZOLL elite-partner relationship; Rettungskette, resQshock and Prominis are mid-market specialists; EventMietservice is a pure event-rental utility; Notfall Akademie is course-led. Pulse4all wedge: predictable monthly price + online activation — currently absent in CH. The clearest white-space of any rollout market.

Top 6 — Switzerland

#1 · 4,000+ Swiss municipalities/firms · Sorglos-Paket

Rettungskette / Reavita AG

www.rettungskette.ch
"Herznotfall? BLS-AED Kurse, Beratung, AED Shop u. Miete vom Spezialisten."
What's bundled
Sorglos-Pakete (Standard for offices/restaurants/pharmacies; Robust for factories/schools/labs): AED, accessories, training, replacement consumables, updates, full service. Monthly fee, no investment.
Messaging themes
rund 4000 Schweizer Gemeinden und Firmen Sorglos-Paket Schweizer Service von Reavita AG Spezialist für Herznotfall
Pricing transparency
Hardware only AED purchase from CHF 1,880-1,890 ex VAT (published); subscription fee quote-only.
Trust signals
'4,000 Swiss municipalities and firms' claim · Reavita AG legal entity · Swiss-only emphasis · Training-first credibility.
Distinguishing features
Two clearly named tiers (Standard vs Robust) · Strong municipal-government share · Hot-line-first contact funnel.
Pulse4all wedge
Their phone-led, German-Swiss tone is a barrier to younger SME buyers. Pulse4all wins with online checkout + flat CHF subscription; emphasise published price vs their 'Sorglos but call us' model.
#2 · Damage & theft insurance unique in CH · multi-brand

resQshock

resqshock.ch
"Defibrillator Schweiz — Hochwertige AED Geräte / Beratung, Verkauf, Schulung, Service & Wartung aus einer Hand."
What's bundled
resQshock AED-Abo (device + accessories + maintenance + training in one package), event/occasion rentals, Damage & Theft Insurance (unique in CH).
Messaging themes
grösste Auswahl für Laien Lieferung direkt aus der Schweiz alles aus einer Hand AED-Abo Schadens- und Diebstahlversicherung
Pricing transparency
Mostly quote Quote-only on Abo; AED purchases priced on product pages.
Trust signals
Manufacturer-agnostic ('largest AED selection for laypersons') · Swiss-delivery emphasis · Multi-language EN/DE site · Partner/cooperation logo wall.
Distinguishing features
Only Swiss provider offering damage & theft insurance · Multi-brand selection vs single-brand competitors · Bilingual UX.
Pulse4all wedge
resQshock's Abo is undefined in price publicly — Pulse4all attacks with a transparent monthly figure + online activation. Match their theft-insurance differentiator by bundling it into the subscription.
#3 · Schweizer Marktführer · 30 yrs · ZOLL Elite Partner

Procamed AG / aed.ch

aed.ch
"Lebensrettende Geräte und Systeme von ZOLL Medical & Mindray — Schweizer Marktführer für lebensrettende Geräte."
What's bundled
ZOLL & Mindray AED sales, full service contracts (free loaner devices within 24h, free updates, discounts, compliance), daily/monthly rental, complete clinical line (CPR machines, ventilators, monitors, infusion).
Messaging themes
Schweizer Marktführer 30 Jahre ZOLL Elite Partner Wartung gemäss Herstellervorgaben Leihgerät innerhalb 24h
Pricing transparency
Hardware only Quote-driven for service/rental · AED list prices visible on shop.
Trust signals
ZOLL Elite Partner status (rare) · 30-year history · Full clinical/professional portfolio · Tier-1 hospital and rescue-service references.
Distinguishing features
Only true ZOLL elite-tier partner in Switzerland · Full medical-device portfolio (not just AED) · 24-hour loaner SLA · CPR machines and patient monitors expand their basket.
Pulse4all wedge
Procamed plays in pro/clinical and won't lose enterprise hospitals. Pulse4all wins SME/non-medical (gyms, hotels, schools, retail) with simpler product, lower price, online ordering. Don't engage on medical credibility; engage on speed and price.
#4 · Medically supervised · Dr. Schild SGNOR · free online course

Prominis

defibrillator.prominis.ch
"Defibrillator Fachhandel Schweiz — Defibrillator mieten oder kaufen, Spezialhandel von Notfallmediziner:innen."
What's bundled
Sales + rental, fully operational packages (battery, electrodes, carrying case, first-aid kit), free online course access for all employees (patient assessment, CPR, AED use), special offers.
Messaging themes
Notfallmediziner:innen ärztlich überwacht Komplettpaket kostenloser Online-Kurs für alle Mitarbeitenden Spezialhandel
Pricing transparency
Single offer Published: ZOLL AED Plus special offer at CHF 1,990 ex VAT. Rental quote-only.
Trust signals
Medically supervised by Dr. Barbara Schild (co-president of the Swiss Society for Emergency and Rescue Medicine), team of professional emergency physicians.
Distinguishing features
Medical-board oversight as primary trust frame (rare) · Free unlimited online training for all employees baked in.
Pulse4all wedge
Prominis's moat is medical-credibility positioning. Pulse4all attacks the segments that don't need the doctor's stamp — hospitality, retail, residential — with a sub-CHF-50 monthly price.
#5 · Most transparent in CH · weekend rental <CHF 100

EventMietservice

www.eventmietservice.ch
"Mieten Sie bei uns alles für Ihren Event — Defibrillator mieten."
What's bundled
DefiSign LIFE AED (full-auto) in backpack with adult + paediatric pads, first-aid kit (mask, scissors, gloves, razor, alcohol wipes); training/instruction add-on.
Messaging themes
für Ihren Event weniger als CHF 100 pro Wochenende praktischer Rucksack kurzfristig kompletter Mietservice
Pricing transparency
Most transparent in CH Full-auto AED CHF 1,200 (purchase ref), rental package <CHF 100/weekend, training CHF 50, accessories itemised.
Trust signals
One-stop event-rental shop (AED is one SKU among many) · Pricing-led trust.
Distinguishing features
Pure event/short-term play · Cheapest weekend rental in CH · Bundled in a broader event-rental basket (no AED specialism, but radical price clarity).
Pulse4all wedge
Not a direct competitor for ongoing protection. Pulse4all should partner-or-ignore: refer event customers, capture the permanent-installation segment EventMietservice never serves.
#6 · BLS-AED-SRC course-led · SGNOR / HF instructors

Notfall Akademie

www.notfall-akademie.ch
"Notfall Akademie Schweiz AG — BLS-AED-SRC Kurse, ZOLL AED 3 Vermietung."
What's bundled
BLS-AED-SRC Komplett & Kompakt courses (Generic Provider for companies; Basic Provider for private), ZOLL AED 3 rental, customised in-house corporate training.
Messaging themes
Notfallmediziner SGNOR Rettungssanitäter HF auf Rettungshelikoptern und Notfallfahrzeugen SRC-zertifiziert Lehrberechtigung
Pricing transparency
Course published BLS-AED-SRC Compact CHF 170; minimum 8 participants; ZOLL AED 3 rental priced on product page.
Trust signals
SGNOR emergency-physician credentials · Helicopter/ambulance instructor pedigree · SRC certification · Course-shop transparency.
Distinguishing features
Genuinely course-led business — AED rental is supportive · Highest-credentialed Swiss instructor team in the segment · minimum-8 group structure forces SME scheduling.
Pulse4all wedge
Notfall Akademie wins training; Pulse4all wins device-as-service. Bundle online training in Pulse4all's subscription so the monthly fee replaces both course-fee + rental on small-team accounts.
