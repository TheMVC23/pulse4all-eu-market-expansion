Compliance-driven market (MPBetreibV, DGUV 204-010, STK inspections). Three heritage B2B players (WERO, Koloszar, Chemomedica) sell quote-led; three e-commerce players (AED-Austria, AED-Defibrillatoren, AEDverkauf) publish prices. No true cancel-anytime subscription exists in the market.

Country snapshot · 60-second read
Austria · top-line
Compliance-driven · 60% quote-led B2B · WERO 'ab 1€/Tag' is the only published rental anchor
Austria's AED reseller market is more fragmented than Sweden's and more compliance-driven (MPBetreibV, DGUV 204-010, STK inspections cited universally). Pricing transparency is moderate — published list prices for hardware are common; service/training contracts remain quote-led. The dominant structure is one-time purchase + separate maintenance contract; rental is gaining ground (WERO "ab 1€/Tag", AED-Partner 12-month tier, Chemomedica Mietoption) but no player offers true cancel-anytime subscription. Pulse4all wedge: collapse the regulatory-comfort + training + service + insurance + device into a single cancel-anytime €30/mo line, undercut WERO's stacked configuration, and out-flex AED-Partner's 12-month minimum.

#1 · DACH compliance specialist · ZOLL/Primedic/Stryker partner

WERO Medical

www.wero-medical.com
"Best in Work.Life.Safety."
What's bundled
Device (ZOLL, Primedic, Stryker) + Defi-Service (commissioning, MPBetreibV functional test, medizinprodukte-buch entry) + WERO AED-Unterweisung (employee training per DGUV 204-010) + service maintenance contracts. Rental from 'ab 1€ pro Tag' (~€30/mo). DACH-wide network of medical-device consultants.
Messaging themes
MPBetreibV-konform DGUV 204-010 deutschlandweit & österreichweit Unterweisung im Kleingruppen ab 1€/Tag mieten
Pricing transparency
Anchor + quote Rental headline 'ab 1€/Tag' (~€30/mo) but actual configurations require quote. Device list prices visible on shop pages.
Trust signals
German engineering positioning · ZOLL/Primedic/Stryker authorised partner · DGUV/MPBetreibV regulatory citations · B2B-only operating posture.
Distinguishing features
Only player explicitly framed around DGUV 204-010 / MPBetreibV compliance in DACH — magnetic for HR/safety officers who need a paper trail.
Pulse4all wedge
WERO sells regulatory comfort. Pulse4all matches the regulatory paper trail (Medizinproduktebuch + Unterweisungsdokumentation included in portal) but at a published flat price with no sales conversation. WERO's 'ab 1€/Tag' headline is misleading at full configuration; Pulse4all's €29.95 is the full configuration.
