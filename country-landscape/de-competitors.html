Germany · Themes matrix & per-player profile

Top 7 DE competitors. Germany is the largest absolute AED market in Europe and the most fragmented — no player owns more than ~15% share. Kroschke matches our €34.95 price exactly but locks 36 months. WERO is the most-cited national distributor but quote-gates rental. Fröhlich Dienste is the closest commercial twin (€29.99/mo · 1-month minimum) but sub-scale. Pulse4all's wedges: B2C/Privatpersonen (empty segment), zero lock-in, transparent TTC price.

Country snapshot · For founders / partners who want the top-line
Largest, most fragmented EU AED market. B2C is empty. Kroschke matches our price but locks 36 months.
Germany has the EU's largest absolute AED revenue (€90-120M/yr, ~20-28K units/yr derived from installed base ÷ 10-yr life + ~6% net-new growth) but no national chain owns >15% share. WERO and Kroschke are the most-cited national distributors — Kroschke publishes €34.95/mo Philips rental (identical to Pulse4all HS1) but with 36-month lock-in. PRIMEDIC and Schiller own "Made in Germany." MedX5 and AED.de win transparent ecommerce. PRIMEROS owns the Erste-Hilfe-Kurs training channel. Fröhlich Dienste is the closest commercial twin (€29.99/mo, 1-month min) but sub-scale. B2C / Privatpersonen is effectively empty — no incumbent targets households with a clean monthly proposition. That's Pulse4all's biggest wedge plus zero lock-in vs Kroschke's 36 months.
