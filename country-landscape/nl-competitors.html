Top 7 competitors

Netherlands · Themes matrix & per-player profile

Top 7 NL competitors. The home market is fragmented (no player >15% share) but every rental competitor — CardiAid, AED Solutions, ProCardio — uses contractual lock-ins of 1 to 6 years. Pulse4all's 3-month opt-out is the only flex-term subscription in NL. Defending that wedge in our most mature market is the central job here.

Country snapshot · For founders / partners who want the top-line
Most mature market in Europe. Most fragmented competitor field. Pulse4all's 3-month opt-out is unmatched.
NL has the densest AED + responder network in Europe (~176/100k registered, 25-43% OHCA survival). The market is ~€18-25M/yr, but no player owns >15% share. Tier-1 webshops (AEDwinkel, AED-Partner, AEDmaster, AEDcompany) compete on transparent purchase price + annual service contracts. Tier-2 rental competitors (CardiAid €35/mo on 72-month lock-in, AED Solutions €1.50/day on 4-8 year lease, ProCardio annual rental) match the lease economics but not the flex term. Pulse4all wedge: 3-month minimum, Philips brand, "we are the loaner" replacement guarantee, B2C-friendly individual-owner sign-up (suits the VvE legal quirk). NL's job: defend the home market against the next subscription entrant.

Our wedge: "Same Philips device, monthly not annual, signed by an individual owner — which is actually what NL property law requires."
