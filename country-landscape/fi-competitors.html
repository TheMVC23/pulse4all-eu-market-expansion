Country snapshot · 60-second read
Finland · top-line
Less mature than DK · pricing universally hidden · Pulse4all is the only player publishing transparent monthly
Finland's market is more institutional and less subscription-mature than Denmark's. Presto dominates the installed base with its "yli 6 500 defibrillaattoria" claim and a workplace-safety contract model that bundles AED into broader fire/safety services. SafeAid and Plusab offer the only true rental/leasing alternatives but both hide pricing behind "request a quote" — a transparency gap. Sydänturva leads on content marketing and bilingual FI/SV support. Tammed wins on device-range breadth and free HD remote training. Kajasafe is a regional north-Finland SME player. Ramirent is short-term event rental only — adjacent, not competitive. Pulse4all's wedge: be the only player publishing €29.95 transparently, on a 1-month rolling all-inclusive subscription, with 24/7 monitoring — undercutting Sydänturva's 1,200-2,500€ upfront, beating SafeAid/Plusab on transparency.

Top 7 — Finland

#1 · Rental specialist · pricing hidden behind email

SafeAid

safeaid.fi
"Defibrillaattori työpaikalle — helppo tapa pitää työpaikan sydänturvallisuutta yllä."
What's bundled
Rental agreement covering phone support, device maintenance, pad and battery replacement during the term, free new pads if used in actual resuscitation. Devices (HeartSine Samaritan PAD 360P ≈ €1,595, Reanibex 100, Philips HeartStart FRx). Training accessories.
Messaging themes
Helppo, luotettava, edullinen Vuokraus työpaikalle Itsetarkistus — ei erillistä huoltoa Uudet elektrodit elvytyskäytön jälkeen 8 vuoden takuu
Pricing transparency
Hidden Device prices published; rental requires emailing info@safeaid.fi for quote.
Trust signals
HeartSine, Reanibex and Philips authorised partner · Long Finnish-language product catalogue.
Distinguishing features
Free replacement pads after actual resuscitation use is explicitly promised — uncommon in FI.
Pulse4all wedge
SafeAid hides rental price behind email — Pulse4all publishes €29.95 on the homepage. Transparency wins SME mindshare. Pulse4all also includes 24/7 monitoring; SafeAid does not.
#2 · 6,500+ AED installed base · Nordic safety distributor

Presto

www.presto.fi
"Defibrillaattori — elintärkeä laite sydänpysähdyksen varalle."
What's bundled
Devices (HeartSine, Zoll, Philips), training, regular battery and pad management, fire-extinguisher and broader workplace-safety services as cross-sell. Self-test devices marketed as 'huoltovapaa' (maintenance-free).
Messaging themes
Yli 6 500 defibrillaattoria toimitettu Suomeen Pohjoismainen turvallisuusasiantuntija Huoltovapaa — itsetarkistus 8 vuoden takuu HeartSine Kokonaisturvallisuus työpaikalle
Pricing transparency
Hardware only Devices publicly priced · service agreements quote-driven for B2B.
Trust signals
'Over 6,500 AEDs delivered to Finland' — strongest customer-count claim in the market. Pan-Nordic safety distributor. Long history.
Distinguishing features
Cross-sell with fire safety — only player who genuinely bundles AED into a workplace-wide safety contract. Largest installed base in Finland.
Pulse4all wedge
Presto sells safety as a category contract to medium-and-large enterprises. Pulse4all targets SMEs and clinics that don't have a Presto-grade safety contract — and offers €29.95 monthly with no minimum, no procurement RFP.
#3 · Content authority · bilingual FI/SV · Vantaa HQ

Sydänturva

www.sydanturva.fi
"Suomen johtava defibrillaattoreiden jälleenmyyjä" — Finland's leading AED reseller.
What's bundled
Wide AED + cabinet range, pad/battery management, huolto (service) and tekninen tuki out of Vantaa. Customer service in Finnish and Swedish.
Messaging themes
Suomen johtava jälleenmyyjä Markkinajohtajan kokemus vuodesta 2012 Suomeksi ja ruotsiksi Hinta 1 200-2 500 € Kilpailukykyiset hinnat ja nopea toimitus
Pricing transparency
Strong on hardware Public price band 1,200-2,500€ for devices, with cabinet adding to total. Service quote-driven.
Trust signals
Founded 2012 — 'market-leader experience' of team · Vantaa technical-support office · Bilingual FI/SV support.
Distinguishing features
Most informative content marketing in FI — long-form articles on AED selection, taloyhtiö (housing-cooperative) cardiac safety. Bilingual SV/FI capability matters in coastal Finland.
Pulse4all wedge
Sydänturva's customer pays 1,200-2,500€ upfront. Pulse4all's €29.95/mo over 36 months = ~1,080€ total all-inclusive. Cheaper and includes pads/battery/monitoring. Pitch: 'Skip the 2,000€ upfront — same outcome, lower over 3 years.'
#4 · Closest analogue to Pulse4all in FI · price hidden

Plusab Finland

www.plusab.fi
"Helppo, luotettava ja edullinen tapa hankkia defibrillaattori."
What's bundled
Rescue Sam AED on leasing/vuokraus agreement including device, free user training, device insurance, maintenance. Companion training (basic resuscitation + AED). HQ in Helsinki.
Messaging themes
Helppo, luotettava ja edullinen Leasing kattaa kaiken — laite, koulutus, vakuutus, huolto Ruotsalaisen elvytysneuvoston sertifioitu koulutus Yrityksille, julkiselle sektorille, taloyhtiöille Helsingin pääkonttori
Pricing transparency
Hidden Leasing terms public ('includes everything'), exact monthly figure quote-driven.
Trust signals
Swedish Resuscitation Council-certified instructors · Helsinki HQ visible · LinkedIn presence.
Distinguishing features
Insurance on the device is genuinely included — covers theft/damage, which most competitors charge extra for or quote.
Pulse4all wedge
Plusab's leasing is the closest analogue to Pulse4all in FI but conceals price. Pulse4all wins on transparency (€29.95 published) and on monthly flexibility (no leasing tie-in). On insurance Pulse4all matches via replacement-on-use, framed differently.
#5 · Broadest device range · HD remote training included

Tammed Oy

www.tammed.fi
"Sydäniskurin asiantuntija Suomessa" — AED specialist in Finland.
What's bundled
Device sales (Philips HeartStart FRx and FR3, Zoll AED Plus and AED 3, Cardiolife AED-3100, HeartSine Samaritan, Mindray, Reanibex). HD multi-camera remote training (€200 value) included free with every AED. Public-event AED rental.
Messaging themes
HD-monikamerakoulutus arvo 200€ sisältyy aina hintaan Laajin valikoima Suomessa Real CPR Help — reaaliaikainen palaute Tapahtumavuokraus Käyttövalmis pakkaus
Pricing transparency
Hardware published Device prices public per product page · rental and service quote-driven.
Trust signals
Authorised reseller for 7+ AED brands — broadest range in FI · Free HD remote-training inclusion is novel.
Distinguishing features
HD multi-camera remote training included in every device price — only Finnish player offering this. Event-rental side business.
Pulse4all wedge
Tammed's pitch is range and bundled training. Pulse4all wins on the lifecycle: training is one event, but the AED needs care for years. €29.95/mo replaces the upfront device cost and ongoing pad/battery/monitoring with one transparent line.
#6 · Regional north Finland · Kajaani-anchored

Kajasafe

kajasafe.fi
"Inhimillinen ja ammattitaitoinen kumppani turvallisen työympäristön varmistamisessa."
What's bundled
Zoll AED Plus on offer (offer pricing on homepage), fire/CO detectors, broader workplace safety products, customised safety training packages.
Messaging themes
Inhimillinen ja ammattitaitoinen kumppani Räätälöidyt koulutukset TARJOUS: Zoll AED Plus Paloturvallisuus ja ensiapu yhdessä Vuosien kokemus
Pricing transparency
Single offer Single device on homepage offer · service and training packages by request from Kajaani office.
Trust signals
Kajasafe Oy registered in Kajaani · Customer reference page (asiakkaitamme) · Zoll authorised reseller.
Distinguishing features
Regional footprint — strongest in northern Finland (Kajaani). Bundles fire + AED + workplace safety training under one regional supplier.
Pulse4all wedge
Kajasafe is regional, transactional and SME-focused but not subscription. Pulse4all enters with a national-coverage all-inclusive monthly that doesn't require a Kajaani-area site visit. We win on geography and on cash-flow simplicity.
#7 · Short-term event rental only · Pan-Nordic rental

Ramirent

www.ramirent.fi
"Defibrillator Rescue Sam — semiautomatic public-use defibrillator that guides the rescuer through CPR."
What's bundled
Day or short-term rental of Rescue Sam AED, alongside their core construction-equipment rental fleet. No training or service bundling.
Messaging themes
Vuokraa lyhytaikaisesti Tapahtumiin ja työmaille Pohjoismainen vuokraketju Sama portaali kuin muulle vuokrakalustolle Nopea palautus
Pricing transparency
Day-rate Rate per day/week visible in catalogue · precise figure on product page (typical short-rental tariff).
Trust signals
Pan-Nordic rental brand · Public-listed parent · Hundreds of branches.
Distinguishing features
Short-term rental only — uniquely positioned for events, festivals, construction sites and one-off needs, not permanent deployments.
Pulse4all wedge
Ramirent serves a different use case (short-term events) — not direct competition. Channel-partner opportunity: customers who rent from Ramirent for an event and then realise they need a permanent AED are a natural Pulse4all referral.
