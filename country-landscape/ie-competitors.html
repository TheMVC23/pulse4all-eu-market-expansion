Country snapshot · 60-second read
Ireland · top-line
GAA programme + PHECC framework define the market · €1-1.50/day rental headlines but device-only
Ireland's AED market is shaped by the GAA defibrillator programme and the PHECC/Irish Heart Foundation accreditation framework. Three players (Hearts.ie, defibshop.ie, Fleming Medical) own the bulk of B2B/community volume; defibshop.co.uk imports UK service-led pricing under "Service Band 3"; Hayes is the e-commerce retailer. Rental headlines cluster at "€1-1.50/day" (€30-45/mo) but device-only — training, consumables, monitoring are separate add-ons. Pulse4all's wedge: match the €30 rental headline but include everything inside it; cancel-anytime vs. 3-/5-year DefibCare or quote-led leasing; replicate the GAA-style community story with a "% to IHF per subscription" hook to neutralise Hearts.ie's emotional brand-equity advantage.

Top 5 — Ireland

#1 · GAA programme · 1,500+ clubs · founder firefighter/paramedic

Heart Safety Solutions / Hearts.ie

hearts.ie
"Saving Lives. Every Day. Everywhere."
What's bundled
Device (HeartSine, LIFEPAK CR2, ZOLL) + LIFELINKcentral / AiviaNet connected monitoring (subscription required) + Aivia Intelligent Cabinets (SigFox network, 99% Ireland coverage) + AED rental from €1.50/day (~€45/mo) + IHF/PHECC-accredited training. National GAA defibrillator programme.
Messaging themes
Ireland's leading GAA National Defibrillator Programme Connected Solutions Intelligent Cabinets Founded 2006
Pricing transparency
Strong rental headline From €1.50/day rental published; device prices on product pages; subscription detail (LIFELINKcentral, AiviaNet) is 'subscription required' without published number.
Trust signals
2006 founding · founder's firefighter/paramedic credentials · GAA partnership (1,500+ clubs) · IHF approved training site · PHECC Recognised Institution.
Distinguishing features
GAA brand association is unmatched in Ireland — 'the company that put a defib in every village' carries enormous credibility. Aivia Intelligent Cabinet on SigFox is unique kit.
Pulse4all wedge
Hearts.ie's €1.50/day rental ≈ €45/mo, before connectivity subscription. Pulse4all at €29.95 includes connectivity, training, consumables. Wedge is price (~33% cheaper) at the same connected-monitoring outcome. The community/GAA story is harder to dislodge — answer with a direct '1% to Irish Heart Foundation per subscription' or similar charity hook.
#2 · €1/day rental price-mirror · sister to UK defibshop

defibshop.ie

www.defibshop.ie
"Independent defibrillator and first-aid training provider."
What's bundled
Multi-brand AEDs + accessories + cabinets + AED rental from €1/day (~€30/mo) entry-level + PHECC-accredited blended first-aid training + occupational health & safety consultancy. Sister company to UK defibshop.
Messaging themes
Independent — impartial advice From €1 a day PHECC Recognised Institution IHF approved Beyond defibs — full OHS consultancy
Pricing transparency
Strong Strong rental headline; per-SKU device pricing visible.
Trust signals
PHECC + IHF + UK parent's 20-year history · broad OHS consultancy reach.
Distinguishing features
Lowest published rental headline in Ireland (€1/day). Bundles AED into broader OHS consultancy — sticky for compliance-driven buyers.
Pulse4all wedge
€1/day = €30/mo on entry-level AED — same headline as Pulse4all but typically device-only with training and consumables sold separately. Pulse4all wins on what's inside the €30: device + consumables refresh + training portal + theft cover, vs defibshop.ie's headline that excludes them. Cancel-anytime vs. annual contract is the second wedge.
#3 · Premium · only formal AED Leasing Programme

Fleming Medical

www.flemingmedical.ie
"Ireland's leading medical equipment & supplies partner."
What's bundled
Device (Schiller, ZOLL Powerheart G5, ME PAD) + defibrillator service + accessories + Fleming Medical Academy online training (CPD-recognised) + AED Leasing Programme (dedicated subpage) + loan/replacement device during service.
Messaging themes
Leading AED supplier AED Leasing Programme Fleming Medical Academy CPD-recognised online training Limerick HQ — nationwide
Pricing transparency
Quote-led Quote-led on leasing; per-SKU pricing on accessories.
Trust signals
Long-established Irish medical-supply group (multi-decade) · CPD authority membership · Schiller and ZOLL distribution · loaner-during-service SLA.
Distinguishing features
Only Irish player with a formal 'AED Leasing Programme' branded as a product. Fleming Medical Academy is a real LMS, not a PDF.
Pulse4all wedge
Fleming's lease ≈ multi-year contract with quote; Pulse4all is monthly self-serve. Fleming Academy is bundled-but-extra on the lease; Pulse4all bakes training into the same monthly fee. Positioning: 'Fleming's leasing programme without the lease, without the quote, without the multi-year minimum.'
#4 · UK serving IE · 'service any AED' vendor-neutral

defibshop.co.uk

www.defibshop.co.uk
"Independent — impartial advice tailored to your requirements."
What's bundled
Buy or rent + DefibCare maintenance & servicing contracts (3 or 5 year tiers) + free annual service for any owner regardless of supplier + e-Learning platform + free reminder service for accessory expiries. Service Band 3 covers Scotland, Ireland, Isle of Man.
Messaging themes
Independent of any manufacturer Impartial advice DefibCare 3 or 5 year Free annual service for anyone Free expiry reminders
Pricing transparency
Strong on hardware Strong on hardware + on DefibCare tiers · rental config quote-led.
Trust signals
20+ years UK trading · 'service any AED regardless of supplier' — vendor-neutral signal · charity/community customer logos on UK site.
Distinguishing features
'We'll service any AED, even one we didn't sell' — only player on this list with that promise. Free expiry-reminder service is a Pulse4all-style touch.
Pulse4all wedge
Defibshop's expiry-reminder is admirable — but it still requires the customer to act on the reminder. Pulse4all just sends the replacement consumable when due. Same for service: defibshop schedules a visit; Pulse4all replaces parts proactively. Cancel-anytime vs. 3- or 5-year DefibCare is the contractual wedge.
#5 · Pure e-commerce · ~10 years online · PHECC instructor team

Hayes First Aid

www.hayesfirstaid.ie
"Ireland's leading online first-aid supplier — for nearly 10 years."
What's bundled
Multi-brand AED shop + cabinets + accessories + on-site PHECC-certified training delivered by in-house instructor team (covers Safety Health & Welfare at Work Act 2005). Customer base: home users, sports clubs, GP surgeries, fire/ambulance services, schools, nursing homes, government bodies.
Messaging themes
Nearly 10 years online Thousands of units supplied PHECC certified instructors Onsite training nationwide Safety, Health and Welfare at Work Act 2005
Pricing transparency
Strong Top-10 AED list with prices on category page · per-SKU pricing throughout.
Trust signals
~10-year track record · PHECC instructor team · broad customer-segment list (sports clubs through emergency services).
Distinguishing features
Genuine generalist first-aid retailer — AED is one of many SKUs. Strong rural/regional reach (Limerick base).
Pulse4all wedge
Hayes is a retailer — sells the box, sells the course separately, doesn't own the customer relationship after dispatch. Pulse4all owns the relationship: monthly billing, scheduled consumables, dashboard. Hayes' SMB customer goes silent after the order; Pulse4all's customer is a recurring contact.
