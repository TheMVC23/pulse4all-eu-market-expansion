Section 01 · Market Analysis

Density gap to NL is the opportunity — 5,000-8,000 AED catch-up

Belgium's installed base (~18-22K including unregistered) yields ~155-190 AEDs per 100k. To match NL (200-250/100k true), Belgium needs an additional 5,000-8,000 AEDs. OHCA incidence is in line with the European average — demand exists, supply has lagged. Wallonia subsidises 75% of AED purchase for sports clubs (CapEx headwind in WAL); Flanders offers no subsidy (subscription tailwind in VLA).

Regulatory drivers — register-then-maintain regime

1
KB / Royal Decree 21 April 2007
Mandatory AED registration in public places
Sets safety standards for AEDs in resuscitation; mandates registration of every publicly available AED with FOD Volksgezondheid. "Public place" defined broadly: shops, schools, business premises, stations, airports, sports fields. Anyone can use an AED without certification.
2
Codex welzijn op het werk
No mandate, but maintenance obligation if installed
Belgian employers are NOT legally required to install an AED. But: if installed, the AED falls under arbeidsmiddelen — documented regular maintenance and inspection are obligatory. Soft tailwind for managed-service models.
3
Wallonia 75% subsidy (sports clubs)
CapEx headwind in WAL only
ADEPS/Région wallonne reimburses up to 75% of AED purchase for sports clubs. Increases CapEx attractiveness — adapt subscription with a 1-time-buy variant in WAL, subscription default in VLA.
4
Flanders Hartveilig label
Subscription tailwind in VLA
Rode Kruis-Vlaanderen Hartveilig program issues "Heart Safe" label to organisations meeting AED + CPR-training criteria. No CapEx subsidy. Pure subscription pitch lands cleanly.
5
Belgium 2025-26 — auto-renewal notification law
First in Europe
Belgium will be the first country in Europe requiring companies to notify consumers of upcoming tacit contract renewal (15 days before deadline). Applies B2C. Adjust subscription comms accordingly.

National foundations — three turnkey co-marketing routes

Bilingual national
Ligue Cardiologique Belge / Belgische Cardiologische Liga
Single national heart-health foundation, NL+FR (liguecardioliga.be). Reanim app (with AEDMAP) maps 145,000+ AEDs worldwide. Heart Knight volunteer-responder network. Week van het Hart annual partnership opportunity.
Flanders flagship
Belgian Red Cross — Hartveilig project
Rode Kruis-Vlaanderen flagship CPR/AED program. "Heart Safe" label for orgs meeting requirements (≥1 AED + 10% staff CPR-trained). Borrowable training mannequins via provincial offices. Wallonia French-speaking Red Cross runs separately — dual-track required.
Federal & registry
FOD Volksgezondheid & B-CAR
FOD runs the registry (aed-dea@health.fgov.be) — not publicly browsable, lists obtainable on request. B-CAR (Belgian Cardiac Arrest Registry) tracks OHCA outcomes. News.belgium publishes AED awareness coverage.

Section 02 · Competitive Landscape

Cardioservice dominates · Pulse4all is the only true cancel-anytime PaaS player

15 mapped suppliers (May 2026). Cardioservice is the dominant national incumbent (15K+ installs). Competing leasing prices land €23-35/mo but all require multi-year commitment and exclude full insurance/swap. Almost no competitor publishes pricing transparently — most are quote-based. Price-clarity is a marketing wedge for Pulse4all.

Key takeaways — Belgium competitive landscape

1
Only true cancel-anytime PaaS
Pulse4all is the only player offering all-in €34.95/mo with cancel-anytime. Defibrion (€23.93/mo), Cardioservice/CardioPro (~€29/mo) leases are cheaper monthly but require multi-year commitment.
2
Cardioservice is the incumbent
15K+ installs. Owns relationships with municipalities, sports federations, copro/syndic via French-language sales force. Pulse4all needs FR-language landing + sales motion, not just a translated NL page.
3
Price clarity is the wedge
Almost no competitor publishes pricing transparently. Pulse4all's bundle (insurance + swap + cancel-anytime + transparent €34.95) is the differentiator, not the headline price.
4
Three turnkey partner routes
Hartveilig label co-marketing (every Pulse4all sub meets criteria) · Reanim app integration (auto-register every unit) · Belgian Red Cross-Flanders training credits bundle.
