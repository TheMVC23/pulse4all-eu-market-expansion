How to read each page

Common structure across all 11 country pages

Each country page follows the SE deck layout — Section 01 (Market Analysis) covers market sizing, AED density, regulatory drivers, segment breakdown, and density vs neighbours; Section 02 (Competitive Landscape) covers the value chain, channel positioning, and a top-10 to top-20 competitor table with pricing and channel data. SE is the gold standard — others go as deep as the available research allows.

Section 01 · Market Analysis
What's the market shape, what's driving it?
Market size estimate (EUR/yr), registered AED count + density per 100k, growth rate, regulatory drivers (mandatory placement laws, workplace safety rules, national CPR/AED bodies), segment breakdown (workplace / public / hospitality / residential / sports / associations), density comparisons vs neighbouring markets.
Section 02 · Competitive Landscape
Who are we up against and how do they reach buyers?
Three-layer value chain (producers → distributors/resellers → end-customer segments). Channel positioning matrix (transactional vs service-led, product-led vs relationship-led). Top 10-20 named competitors with pricing where visible (purchase or subscription/leasing per month), B2B/B2C focus, leasing offer status, training included.
Reference deck
SE deck is the gold standard
The HultbergMayer Pulse4all SE deck (Apr 2026, 45 pages) is the structural reference. Sweden's page rebuilds that depth in HTML. Other markets follow the same layout — sometimes with thinner data where local registries are weaker (no national AED registry exists in many markets — registered counts are estimates).
